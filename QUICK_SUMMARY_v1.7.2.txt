Fans Endpoint:
--------------
GET /api/v1/hardware now appears in both:
- Hardware section (complete hardware info)
- Fans section (focused fan access)

Returns fans-info array with:
- id (integer): Fan identifier
- name (string): Fan name
- min_rpm (integer): Minimum RPM
- max_rpm (integer): Maximum RPM
